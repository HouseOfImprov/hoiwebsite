About

Comedy, plays, musicals, and more - all made up entirely on the spot!

We are a comedy troupe from the University of Oxford who perform "Who's Line is it Anyway?" style improvised comedy sketches and games, as well as full show length improvised comedy plays and musicals. Everything - the plot, the characters, the lines, the songs, and even the sound and lights - is made up entirely on the spot based on sugestions from the audience. We audition for new cast members every year and run free workshops each week of the university term for anyone interested in learning the art of improv.

Workshops

Want to have a go?

We run free open workshops every week of the University of Oxford term, currently at Wadham college. Workshops focus on building core improv skills and are suitable for absolutely anyone who wants to give improv a try. Whatever your background and experience, improv is a fantastic way to develop confidence in front of an audience, express yourself, and have fun. If you'd like to see what workshops are all about, but are nervous about standing up and performing in front of a group, then newcomers are also welcome to join a session just as an "audience member". Contact us to find out more.

Hire Us

We perform at events and run custom workshops

Improvised comedy is an incredibly fun and unique form of entertainment to have at any event. We perform at parties, corporate events, college balls, street festivals, outreach days and more - we're always interested in hearing new ideas. We also offer workshops for all ages and abilities. These can be tailored for team building, public speaking skills, developing confidence, creative thinking, or just for fun! Get in touch with us to discuss what you have in mind.
